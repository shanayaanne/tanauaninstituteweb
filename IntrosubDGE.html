MY DIGITAL WORLD/E-SAFETY

E-safety is often defined as the safe and responsible use of technology.
This includes the use of the internet and other means of communication using electronic media (e.g. text messages, online messaging, social media, gaming devices, e-mail etc).
In practice, e-safety is as much about behavior as it is electronic security. Which highlights the need to educate children and young people about the benefits, risks, and
responsibilities of using information technology.
