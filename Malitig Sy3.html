Alignment of Course Intended Learning Outcomes (CILOs) with Summative Assessment Tasks

Course Objectives Summative Task (Output)
1. Formulate your learning goals and targets in relation to technical drafting

2. Explore, examine, and apply the basic concepts, underlying principles and common competencies in technical drafting specifically using the AutoCAD.

3. Apply the concepts and skills learned in technical drafting

4. Appreciate the relevance of learning the basic concepts and skills of technical drafting.

 Seminar Workshop

Actual demonstration

Sketch and convert

Interview and create

 The learners will check out their prior knowledge about technical drafting. Use this as a springboard to introduce the lesson.

The learners will perform a hands-on activity and they should be properly guided by the teacher

The learner will draw three polygons of their choice in a bond paper. Apply their learning in AutoCAD program, convert it into line segments focusing on the line command.

The learners will interview a draftsman, an architect or an IT student who is knowledgeable in CAD program or application. Ask questions and take down notes. (The teacher may provide questions for the learners to ask.) The learner will create a simple house design using the basics of AutoCAD
