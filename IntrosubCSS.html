CONTACT CENTER SERVICES

This course is designed to enhance the knowledge, skills and attitudes of a trainee/student on core competencies such as; Communicate Effectively Using English Language, Deliver Quality Customer Service, Perform Computer Operation and Internet Navigation, Demonstrate Understanding and Knowledge for the American Culture and Geography in accordance with the industry standards.
It includes common competencies such as; Apply Quality Standards, Perform Computer Operations. It also includes basic competencies such as; Participate in Workplace Communication, Work in a Team Environment, Practice Career Professionalism and Practice Occupational Health and Safety Procedures.
