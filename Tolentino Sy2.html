OBE COURSE SYLLABUS

COURSE CODE
COURSE TITLE/NAME CONTACT CENTER SERVICES
COURSE DESCRIPTION TThis course is designed to enhance the knowledge, skills, and attitudes of a trainee/student on core competencies such as; Communicate Effectively Using English Language, Deliver Quality Customer Service, Perform Computer Operation and Internet Navigation, Demonstrate Understanding and Knowledge for the American Culture and Geography in accordance with the industry standards. It includes common competencies such as; Apply Quality Standards, Perform Computer Operations. It also includes basic competencies such as; Participate in Workplace Communication, Work in a Team Environment, Practice Career Professionalism and Practice Occupational Health and Safety Procedures.
TEACHING HOURS 120 hours
CONTACT HOURS 6 hours per week
COURSE INTENDED LEARNING OUTCOMES (CILOs)
Communicating using the English language Making receiving/making calls to/from customers

Handling complains

Dealing with difficult customers

Providing after-sales report

Preparing documents based on conversation with customers

Perform Computer Operations and internet navigation
